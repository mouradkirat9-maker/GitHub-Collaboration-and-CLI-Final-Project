# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Expected behavior

Contributors are expected to:

- Use respectful and professional language.
- Be considerate of different viewpoints and experiences.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that makes the community unsafe or unwelcoming.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, code, issues, or other contributions that do not follow this Code of Conduct.
